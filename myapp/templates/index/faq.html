How does the subscription and free trial work?

Our app operates on a subscription basis. All new users start with a 7-day free trial period, allowing you to explore the app's features without any charge.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, you can continue to use the app until the end of your current subscription period. No further charges will be applied.

Will I be charged if I cancel during the free trial?

No, if you cancel within the 7-day trial period, you will not be charged anything.

Why does the app sometimes produce nonsensical text from my voice notes?

The app uses AI for speech-to-text conversion, which can occasionally result in errors. If you encounter nonsensical text, we recommend recording your note again to get a correct transcription.

Can the AI insert information that wasn't in my original dictation?

Yes, this is possible. The AI that converts transcription text into structured clinical notes can sometimes produce "hallucinations" - inserting information not present in the original text. We continuously work to minimize this, but it's not perfect. To address this, users can edit all content, including the AI-generated output.

How can I contribute to the app's development?

User feedback is crucial for us. We continuously develop the app and implement features requested by a significant portion of our users. Your input helps shape the future of the app.

Is it safe to record patient information in the app?

For privacy preservation, we strongly advise all users not to record any patient-identifiable data within the app.
